Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the app? I don't see a window.

MindInk is a menu bar app. Look for the waveform icon in the top-right of your screen (the macOS menu bar). Left-click it to open the main window.

How do I start transcribing?

Press your hotkey (default: Option+Space) to start recording. Press it again to stop. The transcribed text is automatically typed into the active application.

Does MindInk send my audio to the cloud?

No. All transcription happens locally on your Mac using AI models stored on your device. Nothing is ever uploaded. The only network activity is downloading models on first use.

Text isn't being typed automatically after transcription.

Automatic text insertion requires Accessibility permission. Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility and add MindInk. Without this, transcribed text is still copied to your clipboard — just paste with Cmd+V.

Which model should I choose?

For most users, Whisper Base (142MB) is a great starting point — fast and accurate. If you need higher accuracy, try Whisper Small or Large V3 Turbo. For English-only dictation, Parakeet CTC is extremely fast.

Can I use MindInk on Intel Macs?

Yes, via Rosetta 2. Apple Silicon Macs will get better performance thanks to Metal GPU acceleration.

How do I uninstall MindInk?

Drag MindInk from Applications to Trash. To remove all data, also delete ~/Library/Application Support/VoiceInk/.
