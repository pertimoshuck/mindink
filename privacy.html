Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

MindInk is a voice-to-text transcription application for macOS that processes all data locally on your device. This privacy policy explains what data MindInk accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

MindInk does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No advertising
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No server-side processing

Audio Data

MindInk accesses your microphone solely to perform voice-to-text transcription. Audio data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device using locally-stored AI models
  • Never transmitted over the internet
  • Never stored permanently — audio buffers are discarded immediately after transcription
  • Never shared with any third party

Transcription History

Transcription results are stored locally on your device in the application's support directory. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is accessible only to you
  • Can be exported or deleted at any time through the app

Clipboard Data

MindInk's optional clipboard manager monitors your system clipboard to provide clipboard history functionality. Clipboard data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted over the internet
  • Deletable at any time through the app

AI Models

MindInk downloads AI models from HuggingFace and GitHub when you first select them. This is the only network activity the app performs. After models are downloaded:

  • No further network access is required
  • The app functions completely offline
  • No data is sent to model providers

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac:

  • AI Models: ~/Library/Application Support/VoiceInk/Models/
  • Transcription History: ~/Library/Application Support/VoiceInk/
  • Clipboard History: ~/Library/Application Support/VoiceInk/
  • Preferences: macOS UserDefaults (local)

Third-Party Services

MindInk does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

MindInk does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
